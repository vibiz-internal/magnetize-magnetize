# Magnetize Competitor Landscape Report

## Executive Summary
The custom photo magnet and keepsake market is dominated by established photo printing giants like Shutterfly and Snapfish, but there are significant opportunities for differentiation. Most competitors focus on standard rectangular magnets with limited customization options beyond photo uploads. There's a clear gap in the market for innovative magnet products, starter kits, and unique keepsake formats that could help Magnetize stand out.

## Major Competitors

### 1. Shutterfly
**URL:** https://www.shutterfly.com  
**Magnet Products:** https://www.shutterfly.com/t/all-magnets/

**What they offer:**
- Standard magnets (3x3, 3x5, 4x5.5 sizes)
- 2x2 magnet sets (4 small magnets)
- Acrylic magnets (2.5x2.5 with polished finish)
- Clip magnets (2.1x2.4 with built-in clip)
- Metal magnets (aluminum, 2x3 rectangular or 4" hexagon)
- Calendar magnets (3x5 or 4x5.5)

**Pricing:** 
- Typically $15-30 for standard sizes
- Regular promotions (40-50% off)
- Free shipping offers on larger orders

**Strengths:**
- Market leader with strong brand recognition
- Wide variety of sizes and styles
- High-quality printing with vibrant colors
- Integrated ecosystem with other photo products
- Mobile app for easy ordering
- Fast shipping options

**Weaknesses:**
- Generic designs and limited customization beyond photos
- Higher prices compared to smaller competitors
- Focus on traditional rectangular formats
- No magnet-specific starter kits or bundles
- Limited creative magnet shapes

### 2. Snapfish
**URL:** https://www.snapfish.com  

**What they offer:**
- Photo magnets as part of their personalized gifts category
- Various sizes and shapes (though less variety than Shutterfly)
- Color-changing magic mugs (not magnets but interesting tech)

**Pricing:**
- Similar to Shutterfly with frequent promotions
- Typically $10-25 for standard magnets

**Strengths:**
- Strong brand presence
- Good quality printing
- Regular deals and discounts
- Easy-to-use design tools

**Weaknesses:**
- Less variety in magnet options compared to Shutterfly
- Magnets are not a primary focus (buried in gift category)
- Limited innovation in magnet formats

### 3. Vistaprint
**URL:** https://www.vistaprint.com  
**Magnets:** Listed under "Postcards & Print Advertising > Magnets"

**What they offer:**
- Business card magnets
- Promotional magnets
- Custom shaped magnets
- Car magnets

**Pricing:**
- More competitive bulk pricing
- Starting around $20 for 25 business card magnets

**Strengths:**
- Excellent for bulk orders
- Business-focused features
- Custom die-cut shapes available
- Fast turnaround times

**Weaknesses:**
- More business/promotional focused than personal keepsakes
- Less emphasis on photo quality
- Limited options for individual consumers
- Not positioned as keepsake products

### 4. Smaller/Specialized Competitors

**Magnets on the Cheap (magnetsonthecheap.com)**
- Focuses on car and business magnets
- Not oriented toward photo keepsakes
- Good for understanding magnet durability/outdoor use

**Local print shops and Etsy sellers**
- Often offer custom magnet services
- More personalized service
- Higher prices, longer turnaround

## Market Gaps & Opportunities

### 1. Starter Kits & Bundles
**Gap:** No competitor offers "magnetization starter kits" that help customers begin their magnet collection.

**Opportunity:** Create themed starter kits:
- New Parent Kit (monthly milestone magnets)
- Family Memory Kit (templates for holidays, birthdays)
- Pet Lover Kit (paw prints, photo frames)
- Travel Memory Kit (destination-shaped magnets)

### 2. Unique Shapes & Formats
**Gap:** Most competitors stick to basic rectangles and squares.

**Opportunity:** Offer creative shapes:
- Puzzle piece magnets that connect
- Speech bubble magnets for quotes
- Shaped magnets (hearts, stars, custom die-cuts)
- Modular magnets that create larger images

### 3. Interactive & Functional Magnets
**Gap:** Current magnets are purely decorative.

**Opportunity:** Create functional magnet products:
- Dry-erase calendar magnets with photo backgrounds
- Growth chart magnet sets
- Recipe card magnets
- Chore chart magnets with photos

### 4. Subscription Service
**Gap:** No competitor offers ongoing magnet delivery.

**Opportunity:** Monthly magnet subscription:
- Automatically create magnets from phone photos
- Themed monthly deliveries
- Family sharing accounts

### 5. Premium Materials
**Gap:** Limited material options beyond standard magnetic backing.

**Opportunity:** Offer premium options:
- Textured finishes (linen, canvas feel)
- Metallic and foil accents
- Eco-friendly materials
- Extra-strong magnets for heavy items

### 6. Digital Integration
**Gap:** Limited tech integration in physical magnets.

**Opportunity:** Tech-enhanced magnets:
- QR codes linking to video memories
- AR-enabled magnets (scan to see animations)
- NFC chips for instant photo sharing

## Recommended Positioning for Magnetize

### Primary Differentiators
1. **"Magnetize Your Memories" System** - Not just magnets, but a complete memory preservation system with starter kits and ongoing support

2. **Modern Design Aesthetic** - Move beyond traditional photo magnets to Instagram-worthy, Pinterest-friendly designs that people want to display

3. **Family-Focused Features** - Tools for creating family timelines, growth tracking, and memory walls

4. **Sustainable & Premium** - Eco-friendly materials with premium feel at competitive prices

5. **Innovation in Format** - First to market with puzzle magnets, AR features, and modular designs

### Target Positioning Statement
"Magnetize transforms your favorite photos into modern keepsakes that tell your story. More than just magnets, we offer complete memory systems with starter kits, innovative shapes, and premium materials that make your fridge, office, or any magnetic surface a canvas for life's best moments."

### Pricing Strategy
- **Competitive Entry**: Match Shutterfly's promotional prices for standard magnets
- **Premium Tiers**: 20-30% premium for innovative formats and materials
- **Value Bundles**: Starter kits at 15-20% discount vs. individual purchases
- **Subscription Savings**: 25% off for monthly subscribers

### Key Marketing Messages
1. "More than magnets - it's your memory system"
2. "From photo to fridge in 3 days"
3. "Starter kits to magnetize every moment"
4. "Premium keepsakes at everyday prices"
5. "The modern way to display memories"

## Action Items for Launch

1. **Core Product Line**
   - Standard photo magnets (match competitor sizes)
   - Innovative shapes (exclusive to Magnetize)
   - Starter kits (3-4 themed options)

2. **Pricing**
   - Launch with 40% off promotion to match competitor standards
   - Free shipping on orders over $35

3. **Key Differentiators to Highlight**
   - Starter kits (unique in market)
   - Modern design templates
   - Fast 3-day delivery
   - Premium materials option

4. **Marketing Focus**
   - Target young families and new parents
   - Emphasize the "system" not just individual magnets
   - Showcase innovative uses (not just fridge magnets)

By positioning Magnetize as the modern, innovative alternative to traditional photo magnets with a focus on complete memory systems rather than one-off products, the company can carve out a unique space in a market dominated by generic offerings.
